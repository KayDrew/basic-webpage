Historic Places in My City

The Union Buildings

Interesting Facts About The UnionBuildings

  • They were designed and built by Herbert Baker and completed in 1913
  • The outdoor arena can accommodate up to 9000 people.
  • The two wings represent English speaking and Afrikaans speaking people of South Africa.

Ditsong Museum

Interesting Facts About Ditsong Museum

  • The museum was founded on 1 December 1892.
  • It is one of the largest natural history museums in the country.
  • There is a vast collection of fish,reptile, mammal and amphibian fossils and skins on display.

Sammy Marks Square

Interesting Facts About Sammy Marks Square

  • The centre was built in neo-Dutch style.
  • It has an annual foot count of 12 million
  • There are 61 retail tenants in the centre.
